# Business Associate Agreement

Common Paper standard Business Associate Agreement (BAA) for HIPAA compliance.

## Overview

This Business Associate Agreement ("BAA") establishes the terms and conditions under which a business associate may handle protected health information (PHI) on behalf of a covered entity.

## Key Provisions

- **Use and Disclosure**: Business associates may only use or disclose PHI as permitted under the Privacy Rule or as required by law.
- **Safeguards**: Implementation of appropriate administrative, physical, and technical safeguards.
- **Breach Notification**: Reporting of any use or disclosure of PHI not permitted under the BAA.
- **Subcontractors**: Requirements for downstream business associates.

## Terms

This agreement covers:
- Business Associate Obligations
- Company Obligations  
- Data Rights & Restrictions
- Breach Notification
- Term & Termination
- Definitions
